Personalised one-to-one sessions

Private sessions are customized to fit you and your needs and the most effective way to get the benefits of yoga. Postures and breathing exercises are selected to fit your own pace and within your own range. The practice is made to fit your body and life experience.

Working one-to-one, we look in detail where you would like to go with your practice. Perhaps you want to learn relaxing poses and techniques that you can incorporate in your daily life. Maybe you want to get hands on practice and guidance to get into more challening postures. Or maybe you feel more happy to practice in a more intimate environment than in a bigger group.

Rediscover and enjoy your natural strength, suppleness, vigor and health through yoga that is developed specifically for you. Deepen your practice, gain confidence, and receive guidance to develop a practice tailored to your individual needs. Personal, private yoga session are the best way to address your goals and work on areas of individual interest.

For new students

As a new student, you may be intimidated by the public classroom environment. Private sessions allows me the opportunity to focus only on you and make sure you are kicking off with an appropriate start. We will go through the basics and I'll make sure you have a correct alignment so you can continue to progress safely in your yoga journey.

For more experienced students

If you are more experienced in yoga, I can help you working on more advanced postures. You will be able to have a deeper insight into specific postures or categories of postures that interest you or that you find challenging. It's the perfect learning environment where I can help you with posture alignment, modifications and can help you develop a balanced home practice. If you are interested we can also learn about yoga philosophy, meditation and breathing practices.
